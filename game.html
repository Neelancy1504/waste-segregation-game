Waste Segregation Game
by ZeroBin

Why This Game?

Waste segregation is a critical step in managing waste effectively and protecting our environment. By sorting waste into the correct categories, we can recycle materials, reduce landfill waste, and minimize environmental pollution. This game aims to educate and raise awareness about the importance of waste segregation in a fun and interactive way.

How to Play:

  • Drag and Drop: Drag the waste items and drop them into the correct bins.
  • Scoring: You earn 10 points for each correct drop and lose 5 points for each incorrect drop.
  • Feedback: After each drop, you'll receive feedback explaining why the item belongs in a specific bin.
  • Goal: Sort all the waste items correctly to complete the game and achieve the highest score!
